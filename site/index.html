FTC Switcher

For technical volunteers and tournament directors interested in live-streaming their FIRST Tech Challenge events, FTC Switcher can help. It acts as a bridge between the FIRST Tech Challenge scoring software and Blackmagic Design ATEM video switchers for hands-free video switching during events.
